# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to providing a friendly, safe, and harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity or expression, level of experience, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive in communication.
- Assume good intent, but prioritize clarity and evidence.
- Accept feedback gracefully and focus on improving outcomes.
- Keep disagreements technical and professional.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, intimidation, threats, or discriminatory language.
- Trolling, personal attacks, or sustained disruption.
- Sharing private information without explicit permission.
- Any conduct that is inappropriate in a professional open-source setting.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing this Code of Conduct and may remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, code, issues, and other contributions that are not aligned with this policy.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, report it privately to maintainers. Reports will be reviewed promptly and handled with discretion.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies in all project spaces, including issues, pull requests, discussions, and other maintainers-managed communication channels.
